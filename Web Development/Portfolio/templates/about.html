03 : About me

Introduction

Hi, I'm Shreyas Viswanathan, a recent high school graduate who will be pursuing Computer Science at Purdue University starting in the Fall of 2023. I began my journey in Python programming during the Fall of 2021 and quickly developed a deep appreciation for its versatility. because of how it can power large-scale web applications and cutting-edge machine learning models. My ultimate goal is to not only contribute to the field as a computer vision engineer, specifically in developing advanced object detection algorithms for autonomous vehicles, but to also improve my ability to tackle algorithmic problems in a systemic manner.

Experience and Skills

I competed in Business Professionals of America's Python Programming division during the 2022-23 school year, achieving state qualifiers after placing in the Top 7 in my school district. Furthermore, I gained valuable experience in building machine learning models through my participation in Inspirit AI's Scholar's program and their Deep Dives: Designing Deep Learning Systems program. These opportunities allowed me to delve into statistical data analysis and explore a range of deep learning algorithms, including GANs and transformers. Collaborating with a team, I successfully developed an Object Detection System and a Reinforcement Learning agent with autonomous driving capabilities.
